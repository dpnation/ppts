About Sara

One coach -
three disciplines

Sara Bresnick brings a combination of credentials that's genuinely hard to find in one person. She started racing professionally in 2002 and competed at the national and international level on the road, in cyclocross and in the mountains.

What sets her apart isn't just the racing background - it's what she built on top of it.

USA Cycling Certified Coach and Level 1 Race Director / Promoter
Masters degree in Physical Therapy, licensed PT
Advanced certifications in biomechanics, bicycle fitting and orthotics
20+ years working with athletes from recreational riders to professionals
