Credentials

Qualifications

USA Cycling Coach

Licensed Physical Therapist

Masters degree in Physical Therapy. Licensed PT with 20+ years of experience treating active people and athletes.

Biomechanics & Bike Fit

Advanced certifications in biomechanics and bicycle fitting. PT-informed approach to position and movement analysis.
